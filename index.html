Frequently asked questions

Answers, without the small print.

Everything you need to know before starting your translation.

What translation services do you offer?

We translate certified documents, legal and business materials, websites, software, marketing content, and technical documentation.

How do you ensure translation quality?

Every project is assigned to a linguist with relevant expertise and reviewed before delivery.

How long will my project take?

Most short documents are ready in 1–2 business days. We confirm the exact date in your quote.

Can you provide a certified translation?

Yes. We prepare certified and sworn translations for official use where available.

Which languages do you support?

Our network covers more than 100 languages, including all major European languages.

How much does translation cost?

Pricing depends on language pair, length, subject, formatting, and deadline. Request a fixed quote.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Files are handled securely and can be covered by an NDA on request.

Can you keep the original formatting?

Yes. We reproduce the structure and formatting of most editable documents.
